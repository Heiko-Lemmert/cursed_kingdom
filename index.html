Controls

“To turn the fate of the Cursed Kingdom, you must first master the art of control. Only those who wield the controller with skill will pass the tests and emerge as a true hero.”

W -> Move Up

A -> Move Left

S -> Move Down

D -> Move Right

F -> Shoot Arrow

Space -> Jump
